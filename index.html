Travel

Nearest airport: DEL (Indira Gandhi International).
Uber is cheap and works well in Delhi. You can, of course, always take taxis but it would be a real shame if you spent some time in India without taking an auto rickshaw. You can order these on Uber too, though you’ll have to pay in cash. Delhi traffic can be slow—please build in buffer time, especially on wedding day.

Stay

We don’t expect guests to make two separate hotel reservations. Our main recommendation is the Jaypee Vasant Continental, but any hotel in South or Central Delhi should be great.

If you’re browsing, we suggest filtering for options rated 8+ on Booking.com (or similar).

Prefer an apartment? One option is Olive Apartments Delhi.

Airbnb neighborhoods that work well: Green Park, CR Park, Panchsheel Enclave, Sarvapriya Vihar, Vasant Vihar, Safdarjung (SDA), or Defence Colony.
